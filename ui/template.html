One text, three hand-made translations

Between the lawyer's sentence and the supervisor's screen, the same regulation is re-interpreted by hand three times: authorities derive validation rules from the text, every bank re-encodes its own reading of the reporting templates, and supervisors reconcile the results. Same words — three private translations, redone at every amendment.

This pilot makes the chain machine-readable end to end — machines draft and apply, a human approves every consequential step.
